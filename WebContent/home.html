Health Advisory for Blood Donation

Before Donation

  • Get a good night's sleep.
  • Eat a healthy meal and stay hydrated.
  • Avoid fatty foods, caffeine, and alcohol.
  • Bring a form of identification.

During Donation

  • Relax and stay calm.
  • Inform the staff if you feel unwell.
  • Avoid looking at the needle if you're squeamish.
  • Squeeze and release the stress ball to keep blood flowing.
  • Keep the donation site clean and dry.

After Donation

  • Rest for at least 15 minutes before leaving.
  • Drink plenty of fluids and avoid heavy lifting.
  • Eat a healthy snack to replenish lost nutrients.
  • Avoid smoking for at least an hour.
  • Contact the blood bank if you experience dizziness or nausea.

Why Donate?

Every two seconds, someone needs blood. Your donation can save up to three lives.

Who can donate

You need to be 18-65 years old, weight 45kg or more and be fit and healthy.

Benefits of Donation

  • Helps reduce the risk of heart disease
  • Stimulates the production of new blood cells
  • Burns calories
  • Free health check-up
  • Feels great to give back!

How often can I donate?

Male donors can donate again after 90 days and female donors can donate again after 120 days.
